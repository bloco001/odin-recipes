Steak

A super easy an delicious dinner to make

Steak is so stupidly easy to make at home. Why pay $30 at a restaurant when you can do it better for $10?

Ingredients

  • Steak
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Butter
  • Literally nothing else
  1. Heat a cast iron skillet up
  2. Throw butter on it
  3. Throw the steak on it for 5 minutes
  4. Flip the steak over for another 5 minutes
  5. Add just a bit of salt and pepper
  6. You're done. Don't do anything else. Don't put ketchup on it like a heathen.
