Lasagna

Does it look good? Let's make our own!

Lasagna is the favorite food of fat cats all over the world. It can be a bit time consuming but once made you can eat it all week.

First up get to the grocery store and get the ingredients.

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • 1 can of Crushed Tomatoes, 2 cans of tomato sauce, 2 cans of tomato paste
  • 2 Tablespoons of Sugar
  • Parsley, Dried Basil Leaves, Salt, Italian Seasoning, Fennel Seeds and Black Pepper
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Parmesan, Mozzarella, Ricotta
  • An Egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving
